Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how FlowWrite ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and that you understand how your data is used.

By using FlowWrite, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Required Permissions

FlowWrite requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • storage - This permission is required to save your preferences and settings, such as your API key, enabled/disabled status, and site-specific settings.
  • activeTab - This permission allows FlowWrite to interact with the content of the webpage you are currently viewing, specifically to detect text fields and provide suggestions.
  • scripting - This permission enables FlowWrite to inject scripts into web pages to detect text fields and provide suggestions as you type.
  • host permissions - Access to web pages is necessary for the extension to function across different websites and provide suggestions in text fields.

These permissions are essential for the core functionality of FlowWrite and are used only for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

FlowWrite collects the following information:

  • User Preferences - We store your extension settings locally on your device, including your AI provider choice, API key, enabled/disabled status, suggestion delay, presentation mode, and list of disabled sites.
  • Text Context - When you type in a text field, FlowWrite temporarily processes the text you've entered to generate appropriate suggestions. This text is sent directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider's API (such as Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic).

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Your browsing history
  • Information from websites you visit beyond what's necessary for generating suggestions
  • Your API keys are stored locally in your browser and never sent to any third-party servers
  • No usage data or telemetry is collected

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the FlowWrite service
  • To generate relevant writing suggestions
  • To improve the extension based on usage patterns
  • To customize the extension according to your preferences

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. The text you type is sent directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider's API to generate suggestions. FlowWrite does not use any backend server - all API calls are made directly from your browser.

Your API keys are stored securely in your browser using chrome.storage.local and are only used to authenticate requests to your chosen AI provider. Your API keys never leave your browser except to make direct API calls to the AI provider you've selected.

We do not have access to your API keys or the text you type. Your data is only shared with the AI provider you choose (such as Google, OpenAI, or Anthropic) according to their respective privacy policies.

Third-Party AI Provider Services

FlowWrite supports multiple AI providers that you can choose from. When you use FlowWrite, your text context is sent directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider's API. FlowWrite acts as a client-side interface and does not intercept or store this data.

Supported AI providers include:

  • Google Gemini - Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI - Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude - Please refer to Anthropic's Privacy Policy

Your API keys are stored locally on your device and are used only to authenticate requests directly to your chosen AI provider. We do not have access to your API keys or text content.

User Rights

As a user of FlowWrite, you have the following rights:

  • The right to disable the extension at any time
  • The right to disable the extension on specific websites
  • The right to clear your stored preferences by resetting the extension
  • The right to uninstall the extension, which will remove all locally stored data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or FlowWrite's data practices, please contact us by:

  • Opening an issue on our GitHub repository
